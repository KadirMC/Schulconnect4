objective

To be the first player to connect 4 of your coloured discs in a line either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.

how to play

  1. The player who plays yellow discs starts the first game.
  2. Players will alternate turns after playing a disc.
  3. On your turn, click the mouse on any of the columns which you want to place your disc into.
  4. The disc will be dropped into the available lowest cell of your chosen column.
  5. Play alternates until one player gets 4 discs of their colour in a line which can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal.
  6. The game can also end without a winner if there is a stalemate.
  7. The player starting the previous game will play second in the new game.

points system

Points are calculated by the number of a winner's moves deducted from 42 which is the total number of cells.

For example, if a player wins with 4 moves, 42 - 4 = 38 points go to the winner.

leaderboard

This site uses local storage of the browser. The results of the game you play in a browser will be stored in your browser local storage and the top 5 results will be shown on the leaderboard.
